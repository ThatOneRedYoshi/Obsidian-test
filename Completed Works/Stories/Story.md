Drasil holds many tales, big and small, across varying time periods and written in many styles.

The main story of [[Tomorrow's Reflection]], is told in an assortment of interconnected stories that all take place in the same world. The Main Stories all take place in the present day, each having their own primary cast of [[Characters]], while characters from other stories may appear, their roles will be minor. Side Stories are smaller stories set in the world, usually pertaining to the lore and history of the world, or perhaps a side story involving one or more characters.

For individual character stories see [[Characters]].

# Fate of the Arcana
Fate of the Arcana is the first main story in [[Tomorrow's Reflection]]. 
The Great Roths Massacre left a looming shadow over the world, and it altered the lives are countless people, past, present and future. Each character will fight for their own sense of justice, trampling over each other to accomplish their goals, even though all are facing a common enemy. 
## Chapter 0: Before Fate Sets In
Chapter Zero is the prologue chapter of Fate of the Arcana, taking place before the main story it consists of both character backstories and stories taking place before the main events.  This chapter exists to build and develop the cast, explaining their motivations and goals.
