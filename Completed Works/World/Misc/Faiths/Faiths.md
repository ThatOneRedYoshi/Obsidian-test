Drasil hosts many religions, cults, faiths and beliefs all stemming from different walks of life, experiences and teaching. Some believe in a particular deity or figure, while others' faith lies in concepts or objects. Many people revolve their entire life, morals and system of belief around their respective faith, while others may practice in casually. Faiths have existed since the old world, however much new information about the world has shifted the views of much of the populous, changing the common beliefs of people as we know it.

# Types of Faith
There are many different types of faith, however they can usually be broken down into two distinct categories.

## Religions
Religions are recognized groups of individuals that hold their faith into some form of higher power, where that be spiritual, physical, metaphorical or any other kind. Religions typically practice certain moral, ethical and social beliefs and may enact certain actions to reflect that. Many religions place their faith in certain deities or higher powers and often have places of worship.

## Cults
Cults exhibit similar traits to religions, however, their key difference lies in ethical practice. Where religion is typically practiced privately and safely, without affecting the lives of others, cults are known to be harmful, practicing beliefs that may threaten or endanger others.
