When a person's physical form, mind and soul separate, they become what is known as a spirit. Spirits exhibit many traits, and come in many forms, but the common trait of a spirit is that they are all considered or originate from the deceased. There are numerous types of spirits, exhibiting many traits.

# Haunting Spirits
Haunting Spirits refers to spirits that originate from deceased humans or [[Metasoma]]. Haunting Spirits are also commonly referred to as 'Undead' as to become a Haunting Spirit means that one has been unable to pass on after death. Few instances have existed however, causing some to return from the dead without becoming spirits, these people are simply labelled as "undead" and often suffer from symptoms like memory lost and episodes of mania.
