# City
Hadron City is a relatively small city with a population of roughly 100,000 people. It is constructed mostly of grey concrete buildings and metal warehouses. Much of the city is dedicated to study and many of the buildings are used as testing facilities and storage. The homes are usually one story homes that fit two or three people at most. Streets are quite compact in [[Hadron]] and there are only a select few SUV vehicles used for transport, while large cargo trucks are used to transport goods. Hadron City is also home to [[Hadron University]], which has its own campus area.
