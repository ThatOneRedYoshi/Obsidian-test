The Shrine of Kulala is a location in [[Folia]].

The Shrine of Kulala is a triangular shrine found in the city of [[Folia]] and used by people of the [[Oneiros]] faith. It is supported by strong circular pillars. The inside is a square building with a soft red carpet that is used for meditation and rest, in the very centre of the building is a rounded technological terminal that is used for the input and archiving of knowledge received through meditation. The walls have inscribed images of a divine with many hands cupping the head of their worshippers and bringing them knowledge. Each sitting place is suspending by two steps as a way for the people to get closer to their deity.
