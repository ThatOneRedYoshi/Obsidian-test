Metam Theatre is a building located in [[Lampsi]].

Metam Theatre is a colossal sized dome in the centre of Lampsi, it is considered the holy place for all things artistic. Known for its red, blue, white and gold colours, the gigantic dome is surrounded by a more linear external building. The inner dome and outer building are separated by an inner garden, used to showcase the fine work of the nation's greatest florists. The outer building acts as an art museum, exhibiting the finest art of the last year, including but not limited to, paintings, drawings, sculptings, literature, photography and so on. The inner dome is a gigantic theatre, with an undeterminable maximum capacity, though it is estimated to be able to hold around seven hundred thousand people. The Metam Theatre dome is used to showcase all forms of performances and motion picture, including but not limited to, theatre, movies, animation, musical performance and so on.
