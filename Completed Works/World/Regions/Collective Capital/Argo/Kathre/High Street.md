The High Street is a location in [[Kathre]].

[[Kathre|Kathre's]] high street is a bustling street found at the heart of the city with an immense shopping district found within. The High Street specialises in exclusive, authentic goods either found within [[Kathre]] or imported from far away regions, as such, the goods purchased from here are extraordinarily rare and are used as a sign of status amongst the citizens of not only [[Kathre]], but the entire [[Collective Capital]].
