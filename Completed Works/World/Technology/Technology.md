With the introduction of [[Magic|Magic]] has come many innovations in technology and science. Many different functions of magic can be found throughout the systems of [[Drasil]]. Magic technology is defined as technology that either utilises the magic of a person or object in its functions, or use mana as a power source

There are various types of magic technology 

[[JUNC-Corp|JUNC-Tech]] is a notable manufacturer of magic technology, creating and selling products to the capital

# Magic Stasis Technology
Magic Stasis Technology is a form of device made from the magic of another person, typically allowing the functions of their magic to persist in place without their direct input, usually through sacrificing a part of their mana. Magic Stasis Technology requires a mana supply to operate and will cease function should it run out

- [[Magic Gates]]

# Magic Built Technology
Magic Built Technology is technology that is built from objects imbued with magic. often using said objects for both power and function. Magic Built Technology is very common in cities as many are built with magic technologies.

# Magic Functioning Technology
Magic Functioning Technology is a device that does not require magic to operate, but is instead built to harness magic. Commonly attributed to weapons, Magic Functioning Technology is built as a catalyst to use other objects imbued with magic. Many common day to day objects for under this category, especially industrial and commercial machinery.
