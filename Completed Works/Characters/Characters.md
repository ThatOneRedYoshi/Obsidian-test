[[Drasil]] is home to people of all walks of life, each with their own stories, mysteries and abilities unique to them. Each individual within Drasil wields at least one of the six known types of [[Magic|magic]]. Some people who live in Drasil are also a sub-species of human known as a [[Metasoma]] and others are [[Spirits]].
