# Profile
Scarlet is one of the four puppet's that protects [[Maria]], it is a [[Magical Beings|magical being]] that seems to be created from from Maria's magic.

Scarlet is Maria's primary guard and the most ruthless of her puppets, it spends most of its time training in combat.

## Weapon
Scarlet’s Weapons, Misery and Torment, are a pair of non identical dual blades comprised of various animal parts, Misery is a straight blade with sharpened edges with a human skull foregrip, the handle is comprised of an unknown animal spine and the blade itself is made of a mutated woodpecker beak, the second blade Torment is a dual head blade, the head is created from a ribcage with the blades being made of the same woodpecker beak as Misery, the handle is comprised of snake scales with the bottom being a severed snake head

## Appearance
Scarlet wears black ripped jeans with a red inner fabric, a plain black shirt with an overly designed skull and large block heeled shoes with an excessive black and red design on them, it also has an excess of spiked accessories.

Scarlet is tall (6,0), relatively skinny and made of a pale white fabric, it is quite flexible given its height and is able to bend and contort its limbs with ease, it has a noticeable gash in its stomach revealing a part of its skeleton, it has a very traditionally emo black and red haircut with one side covering its eye

Scarlet is a very over the top take on the emo stereotype of character. Based off of her brother's mellow, expressive demeanor, Scarlet’s design is meant to represent Maria’s ability to weaponize her emotions into a powerful tool to manipulate and deceive her opponents, its sharp and hostile appearance being used to express Maria’s avoidance of people

# Personality
Scarlet is quiet and reserved, saying very little and letting his actions speak instead. When Scarlet does speak it is often short sentences and is only to exchanged information.
