Kariwa Hadron is a historical character in [[Tomorrow's Reflection]].

# Profile
Kariwa Hadron was a member of the [[source-folder/Completed Works/Characters/Groups/Notable Groups/Hadron Family]] and the grandmother of [[source-folder/Completed Works/Characters/Side Characters/Nicklas Hadron]], the [[Kariwa]] region was named after her.

# History
Not much is known about Kariwa Hadron, aside from that [[source-folder/Completed Works/Characters/Side Characters/Nicklas Hadron]] named the [[Kariwa]] region after her.
