Elena Hadron is a historical character in [[Tomorrow's Reflection]].

# Profile
Elena Hadron was a member of the [[source-folder/Completed Works/Characters/Groups/Notable Groups/Hadron Family]], she was in charge of [[Brilth]], one of the six major cities of [[Hadron]]. 

# History
Elena inherited [[Brilth]] from her father at a young age, not much is known about her leadership but it can be assumed that her rule was not very effective, as many cracks existed in Brilth's security. One of these cracks lead Elena's youngest child to get kidnapped by a proxy of the [[source-folder/Completed Works/Characters/Groups/Notable Groups/Lopnur Family]], which they used to blackmail her into handing over her resources. The fate of Elena and her child remain unknown, but the [[source-folder/Completed Works/Characters/Groups/Notable Groups/Lopnur Family]] remain a hold on Brilth to this day.
