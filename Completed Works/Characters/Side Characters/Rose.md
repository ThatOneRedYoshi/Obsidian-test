This document is about Rose, [[Maria|Maria Lavaux's]] puppet. You may be looking for the character [[Rose Lavaux]].

# Profile
Rose is one of the four puppet's that protects [[Maria]], it is a [[Magical Beings|magical being]] that seems to be created from from Maria's magic.

Rose is Maria's favourite of her puppets, however, she still shows a lack of empathy towards it, calling it "Pathetic" when it goes down in combat.

## Weapon
Rose’ Weapon, Ego, is a large Scythe made of mostly human body parts, the handle is made of flesh from what appears to be the liver, the arm clutch is made of bone from what appears to be the spine the bottom is a sharpened forearm bone and the head is made of what looks like a bird skull, the inner mouth is created entirely of gums which when plucked sound like Guitar strings, this tuning changes depending on how wide the mouth is opened, the hilt and head also both have eyes, the lower eye appearing to be a human eye and the primary eye appearing to be the eye of either a bug or a raven.

## Appearance
Rose wears a black shirt that has been cut around the lower middle removing the bottom middle  with noticeable cuts, one of the shoulders on its shoulders is also loose over the top, it wears a medium length pink and black skirt with pink fishnets and then black chained boots, lastly it has a pink rose in its hair

Rose is average in height (5,8) quite bulky and is made of a bright peach coloured fabric, its arms are notably long and lanky and it has a large red eye engraved into its neck, it has medium length pink and black hair with a noticeable punk vibe to it, it also appears to be wearing pink lipstick and black eyeliner however this ‘makeup’ is just a part of its body

Rose is a reflection of Maria’s ego and pride, its rebellious style of clothes reflecting its sarcastic personality. Based heavily on Maria’s mother in terms of hairstyle and colours, Rose’s punk rock design is made to capture her rebellious and rude personality.

# Personality
Rose is sarcastic and rude, it often acts as Maria's spokesperson, loaning out insults to people that it doesn't like.
