# Profile
Autumn Lavaux is [[Maria|Maria Lavaux's]] deceased father, first appearing in [[Emptiness of the Lake (Maria Chapter)|Emptiness of the Lake]] as a supporting character. Very little is shown of Autumn's personality, though Maria seems to remember him as having an air of authority. Several points in the story indicate that he worked hard to support his family.

# History
There is near nothing known about Autumn's past, at some point he both met [[Rose Lavaux]], got married to her and had two children and outside of that has lived to support his family ever since. During the invasion of [[Stheno Roths]], he is shown to be the first to throw his life away so that his family can escape.
