"Shadow" is a nickname for an unnamed character who appears in [[Tomorrow's Reflection]].

# Profile
Shadow's first appearance was in [[The Gambler and the Princess (Pandora Chapter)]]. 
She is shown to be sadistic and somewhat unstable, having a short temper and a blatant disregard for life. She appears to work for a group that have been nicknamed [[To Be Added Documents/Characters/Groups/Lead Factions/The House of Cards|"The House of Cards"]].
Shadow often says "Ya" at the end of sentences or in place of "Yes"

# History
She showed up randomly one night at [[Pandora|Pandora's]] room in the [[Glaspalast]]. She was seemingly trying to recruit Pandora to [[To Be Added Documents/Characters/Groups/Lead Factions/The House of Cards|"The House of Cards"]]. She demonstrates her short temper when she murders [[Ida]] in cold blood. After being unsuccessful to recruit Pandora, she leaves, giving her what appears to be a blank Tarot Card.
