# Profile
Autumn is one of the four puppet's that protects [[Maria]], it is a [[Magical Beings|magical being]] that seems to be created from from Maria's magic.

Autumn is often found trying to entertain Maria, with very little success. It acts as Maria's servant, doing menial tasks for her that she does not wish to do.

## Weapon
Autumn’s Weapon, Pessimism, is a very simple book encased in a Zebra ribcage, the contents of each page reference various organic based spells. There will be 13 spells each with 6 variations each spell creating a different sound

## Appearance
Autumn wears a sharp green suit with aviator glasses and a large belt around its waist as well as a white under-shirt, it also carries a thick book on its left hip in a pouch attached to its suit, its tie is thin and sharp fitting to the rest of its outfit

Autumn is tall (5,9) and noticeably masculine in appearance, it has a pale white fabric and is bulky in appearance, it is more often that not seen floating as opposed to walking, is has a very 1800s hairstyle which is black with green streaks, it has cuts across its leg revealing small pieces of bone however these are usually hidden by its outfit

Autumn’s classy and uptight appearance is meant to represent Maria’s showmanship, her desire to be above others and be seen as the princess she sees herself as. Autumn’s design being based off of the only authority Maria knew, that being her father.

# Personality
Autumn is polite and formal, but has a strong sense of humour and often tells joke that fall flat. It also has a strong sense of duty, and does whatever it can to help Maria in ways both big or small.
