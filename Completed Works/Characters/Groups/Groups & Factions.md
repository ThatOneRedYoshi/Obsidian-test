This entry is for the groups and factions of Drasil. For religions and cults see [[Faiths]].

Countless Organisations, Factions, Groups and more all exist in [[Drasil]], often holding notable influences or power. All groups in Drasil seem to align themselves with one of the major factions struggling for power over Drasil.

The Major factions of Drasil all work against each other and have their own independent goals.
