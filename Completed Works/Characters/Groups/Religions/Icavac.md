The Icavac Faith is a religion found in [[New Wave Ocean]].

# Description
The Icavac are a more faithful iteration of the [[Sirens]] of [[New Wave Ocean]]. Originating when the sirens first discovered the [[Uasivi Vatu]], the sirens pray to it, believing it to be a gift from the divine.

# History
When the Sirens returned to [[Koro' Ni Vale]] after being outcast from human society, the mysterious rock they brought with them brought them great benefit ass a society, protecting them and allowing them to prosper by fending off predators and enhancing the mana flow around their homes, leading them to discover [[source-folder/Completed Works/World/Nature/Natural Resources/Plants/Calmea]].
The sirens have prayed to the Uasivi ever since, and whether by coincidence or through it answering their prayers, they have not encountered any threats to their kind since.

# Traditions
Traditional practice of the Icavac people is to tattoo intricate patterns on their tails, conveying a language that only they can read. They also wear earrings made from red gemstones that they carve into perfect orbs to mimic the shape of the Uasivi Vatu.
