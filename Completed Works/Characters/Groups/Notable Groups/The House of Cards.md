The "House of Cards" is the unofficial name for a group in [[Tomorrow's Reflection]].

# Description
The House of Cards is an infamous organisation that has been seemingly making moves across Drasil for the last few decades. It's member are recognized by their calling of a blank tarot card, their motivations and goals are a complete mystery but its members do not appear to get along.

# History
The "House of Cards" history is a complete mystery, nothing is known about them, but they appear to be making moves towards those they call the "Likened".
