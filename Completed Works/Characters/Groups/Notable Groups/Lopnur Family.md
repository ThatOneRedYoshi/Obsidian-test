The Lopnur Family is a [[Groups & Factions|group]] found in [[Tomorrow's Reflection]].

# Description
The Lopnur Family is ruling family of the [[Lopnur]] region. They are a military oriented family that are extremely loyal to the [[Collective Capital]] and [[Roths Family]].  

# History
Originally a military/political family that got their start with as politicians in the [[Collective Capital]], their presence increased after the splitting and expansion of the [[Experimental Zone]],  when the Lopnur family were instated alongside the [[source-folder/Completed Works/Characters/Groups/Notable Groups/Hadron Family]] to rule over one of thee three regions, the [[Lopnur]] region. Their family have ruled over the region ever since and have remained highly loyal to the [[Roths Family]], though they have their own agenda and seek power in their own means they have always stayed in line with the wishes of the Roths.
