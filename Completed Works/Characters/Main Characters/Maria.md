**Description**: A girl adrift in a lonely ocean never to love again, an ambition of only vengeance. Will she be able to cut the strings binding her to the past or will she forever be puppeted by her emotions?

Maria lives with Chronos, the individual who kindly took her in after awakening one hundred years after her supposed death alongside her family at the hands of Stheno Roths. Life with Chronos is calm and quiet, a stark contrast to the lively energetic household she grew up in, Maria is left relatively undisturbed by Chronos, she is quite accustomed to this loneliness as no bond she could create will ever return to her the joy she used to have, Chronos respects her wishes to be alone and does not pester her more than needed, however Maria has an unhealthy habit of sneaking out of the house from time to time in her pursuit of vengeance and thus Chronos has to intervene, this tradition replays every few days or so in a vicious cycle of scolding and ignoring, between the times of these rituals Maria is studying on her magic and testing her own endurance, her behaviour is self-destructive and irrational but her blind sighted hunt for revenge will not be halted by trivial factors like rationale or self care.
