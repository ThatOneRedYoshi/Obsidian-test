# Design Information
## Outfit
Voila has two variations of her outfit depending on whether she is in Siren or Human form. In both forms she wears a frilly, off-shoulder blouse cropped around her stomach area, it has detached sleeves with frilly cuffs with feathers coming off of it and drapes that extend own from the elbow, both the sleeves and top are coloured in a gradient starting out as a pastel pink extending down to a pastel blue, it is covered in white star patterns all across. The drapes of the sleeves extend down, they are the same gradient colours as the top with a similar pattern to the top, the end of the drape has a sun pattern, around her neck she also wears a beaded necklace with a twelve pointed star pendant. With her human form, in place of where her tail would be she wears a blue skirt that extends down to her ankles with an intricate geometric pattern at the bottom and light blue high heels with a ribbons on either side just under each ankle with a clip attaching the ribbons to the shoe of an upside down star.    

## Appearance
Voila is physically average in height and body (5,8) with a slight but noticeable amount of muscle in her arms, she has a light tan on her face with a mole on her left cheek and ruby red eyes, she has a large mermaid tail that has a fading blue pattern, getting darker as it reaches the bottom, covered furthermore in glittery star patterns. Additionally there is cut sections on several parts of the ends of her fins, giving them a more triangular shape. She has triangular shark-like teeth and wears a thin amount of pink and blue eyeshadow. She has two tattoos, a hand tattoo depicting the constellation of Pisces and a back tattoo depicting an upside down star. Finally, her hair is blonde, medium length, with pink highlights around the bottom.

Voila's design is intended to be a little misleading, her design seems friendly on the surface but there are small red flags all across that indicate something is a little amiss. Predator like aspects such as her shark teeth are extra indicators of this.
